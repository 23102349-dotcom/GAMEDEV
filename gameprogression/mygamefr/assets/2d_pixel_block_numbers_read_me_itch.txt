About
-----
A selection of block number sprites designed for use in GBStudio.


Contents
--------
16 variants of numbers at 8 x 16 size.
5 variants of numbers at 8 x 8 size.


Format
------
All in png format and sharing the gbstudio 4 color palette.
Aseprite format files for the above.
